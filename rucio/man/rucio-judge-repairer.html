Daemon rucio-judge-repairer¶

The Judge-Repairer daemon is responsible for the repair of stuck replication rules.

usage: rucio-judge-repairer [-h] [--run-once] [--threads THREADS]

Named Arguments¶

--run-once

One iteration only

Default: False

--threads

Concurrency control: total number of threads for this process

Default: 1
