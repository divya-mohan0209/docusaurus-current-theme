Daemon rucio-cache-consumer¶

Rucio Cache Consumer is a daemon to add/delete cache replicas to Rucio catalog.

usage: rucio-cache-consumer [-h] [--num-thread NUM_THREAD]

Named Arguments¶

--num-thread

Concurrency control: threads number

Default: 1
